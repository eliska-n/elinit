I am Eliška, a Czech girl who decided to learn to code during the misery of covid pandemic lockdown.

Elinit is 50/50 my personal website and a platform, where anyone can become a blogger!

Why is it billingual?

English is the language for coding and presenting myself in the community. However, I simply love my native language and I couldn't resist writing my posts in Czech. And also... You know... I wanted to show it to my mom. Proper localization of the website brought two problems – I cannot do it technically, and my Czech stories don't reflect the English presentation perfectly. Improvement is on my To Do list.

Why Elinit?

I have bought elinit.cz domain for one of Czechitas lecture. I didn't know, whether I would ever use it. Thus, I invested just few minutes to creating the name. It is my personal homework on Django. Even though there are many troubles, it is my first programming project and I am very proud seeing it online. Have fun with me, cause that's the only reason for this website.

The way it was created and what's next?

If you were interested in coding troubles of one Czech blondie and people who helped her, and platforms that gave her all the education, read through my first blog post. In my second post you can learn about all things that work and many more that do not on this website. If you were willing to help me with debugging, I would be greatly grateful for your comment, help or just words of comfort.
I plan to post all project on Github when it is ready.
If you wanted to become a blogger on this unicorn-full website, I would be the happiest developer! Please don't be frustrated by broken features and non-existent support. It's supposed to be fun and I'd be delighted if someone would share it with me.
I would like to add more features to the page. This will depend on my further development and direction. For now, it's an idea and a resolution. Let's be surprised what life brings.
